Minotaur

The Minotaur is a beast who is part man and part bull (usually depicted as a man with a bull's head).
He lived inside the Labyrinth created by Daedalus. He was imprisoned there due to his savage nature and
thirst for human flesh.
The Minotaur was finally killed by Theseus when he was sent into the Labyrinth as a sacrafice.
