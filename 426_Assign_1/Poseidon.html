Poseidon

Poseidon is the one of the three eldest gods, the other two being:

  • Zues, ruler of the sky
  • Hades, ruler of the underworld

Poseidon is the ruler of the seas, earthquakes and horses. He was worshipped by many of the seafaring cultures of Greece.
He is perhaps most well known for being the diety that was responsible for Odysseus' journey in the Odyssey, cursing Odysseus
after he blinded the cyclops Polyphemus, one of Poseidon's children.

He is also well known for being the divine father of Theseus, the demi-god
who slayed the Minotaur in the Labyrinth.
